Jujutsu Kaisen

Action Supernatural Dark Fantasy 8.8 ★★★★☆

Synopsis

When high school student Yuji Itadori swallows a cursed finger belonging to the King of Curses, Ryomen Sukuna, he becomes the host of an incredibly powerful and malevolent cursed spirit. Recruited by Satoru Gojo, the strongest jujutsu sorcerer alive, Yuji enrolls at Tokyo Jujutsu High to learn how to control cursed energy and fight cursed spirits that threaten humanity. Alongside his classmates Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji must consume all of Sukuna's fingers to contain the threat — even if it means sacrificing his own life. With jaw-dropping animation by MAPPA and a dark, unpredictable story, Jujutsu Kaisen has become one of the defining anime of its generation.
